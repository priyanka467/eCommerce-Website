ARTISAN'S PROFILE

Meet Kishore Malhotra, a renowned Rajasthani potter with over 40 years of experience in the art of pottery. Based in the vibrant village of Khuri, Kishore is known for his exquisite hand-crafted ceramics that reflect the rich cultural heritage of Rajasthan. Kishore’s workshop is a hub of creativity, where he often mentors young artisans, sharing his expertise and passion for the craft. His work not only serves as functional art but also tells stories of the region’s history and traditions. With a keen eye for detail and a dedication to preserving Rajasthani pottery, Kishore is not just a craftsman; he is a custodian of a timeless art form.
